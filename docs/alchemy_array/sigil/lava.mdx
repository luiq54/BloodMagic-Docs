The sister sigil to the $(l:bloodmagic:alchemy_array/sigil/water)Water Sigil$(/l). Press [$(k:use)] when holding the $(item)Lava Sigil$() to create a source block of $(fire)lava$() on the ground, for the cost of $(blood)1000 LP$(). Crafted in an $(l:bloodmagic:alchemy_array/functional_arrays/arcane_ash)Alchemy Array$(/l) using a $(item)Lava Reagent$() and a $(item)Blank Slate,$() it'll drain 5 hearts from you if you don't have enough LP in your $(l:bloodmagic:altar/soul_network)Soul Network.$(/l)
