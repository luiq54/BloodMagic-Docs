---
title: Teleposition Sigil
sidebar_position: 0
---


Teleports the user to a linked Teleposer at a cost of $(blood)1000 LP$() per use. By shift-clicking on a Teleposer with the sigil in hand, the location and dimension of the Teleposer can be recorded, allowing for a quick escape back home. $(br2)Just don't move the Teleposer by accident...
