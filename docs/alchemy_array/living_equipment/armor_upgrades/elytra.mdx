Adds an Elytra to your Living Armor. Rather than being trained, the $(l:bloodmagic:alchemy_array/living_equipment/living_tomes)Upgrade Tome$() must be crafted. $(br2)This Elytra does drain durability from the Chestplate, but at half the speed of a normal Elytra. $(br2)It also looks pretty neat.
