The $(item)Rune of Augmented Capacity$() increases the capacity of the $(l:bloodmagic:altar/blood_altar)Blood Altar$(/l) by a multiplicative +7.5% per rune. The Augmented Capacity runes apply $(o)after$() the regular $(l:bloodmagic:altar/blood_rune/capacity_rune)Capacity runes$().
